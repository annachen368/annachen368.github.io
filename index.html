Improved performance for contact loading and service calls when handling 2000+ contacts

The contact list screen triggered all service calls at once on launch, causing performance issues. I optimized it by introducing coroutine-based pagination, debounced user input, and a Mutex lock to prevent duplicate network calls. This improved load time and scroll smoothness for large contact sets.
